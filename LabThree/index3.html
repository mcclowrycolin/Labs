Who Would Win? Gorilla vs. Grizzly Bear?

Gorilla Facts

  • The average weight of a male Silverback Gorilla is about 400 pounds
  • A gorilla can run up to speeds as high as 20 mph
  • Gorilla's have opposable thumbs
  • Gorilla's are much better on two feet than a grizzly

Grizzly Facts

  1. The average weight for a male Grizzly Bear can be 700 pounds or more
  2. Grizzly bears can reach speeds of up to 35 mph
  3. Grizzly Bears have 20 sharp claws
  4. Grizzly Bears have a much more aggressive demeanor
