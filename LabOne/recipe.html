English Muffin Mini Pizza's

Ingredients

  • English Muffin
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Dried Oregano

Instructions

  1. Break apart your english muffin and toast both sides
  2. Spread pizza sauce over both halves of yourr english muffins
  3. Add Mozzarella cheese over the sauce on each english muffin halve
  4. Add a singular pepperoni to each english muffic, over the mozarella
  5. Sprinkle oregano ovr each english muffin
  6. Microwave for 50 seconds
  7. Let cool for 2 minutes and enjoy
